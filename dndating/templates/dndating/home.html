About Us...


Dungeons & Dragons is a fantasy tabletop role-playing game . D&D departs from traditional wargaming by allowing each player to create their own character to play instead of a military formation. These characters embark upon adventures within a fantasy setting.


This website was created so players all around the world can form the perfect party and start an epic adventure!!!!. How to navigate:

  • Fisrt you create a profile in the Create Profile category. You can input some information about yourself like the class, edition you want to play and a small discription and an image.


  • Then move to the Meet friends category were you can find the perfect party to team up. By clicking on the profile link you can see all the information that they put as well as and ways to communicate via facebook, instagram or discord!


  • You can also edit your profile by clickng the edit button on the Meet friends category. You can easily find your profile by searching for it in the search bar.



  • If you are curently in a dnd group you can disable your profile by selecting the Close profile button located in the bottom of the edit page. When you are ready again to go into a new adventure you can rush into battle by creating a new profile.


And it is that simple! Now you can jump right into action with your perfect party of adventurers and have in your side great warriors and good friends!! Have fun new adventurer
